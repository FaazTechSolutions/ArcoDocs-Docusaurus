# Pay Period

Manages monthly payroll periods linked to a specific PayCycle ID.

### PayCycle IDs:

**1-30-Business**

  - **Period:** 1st to end of the month
  - **Cutoff date:** 20th of the month

**IndPC-for Individual**

  - **Period:** 1st to end of the month
  - **Cutoff date:** end of the month
